About Us
Welcome to My Moving Pal! I’d like to share a little background into our company and purpose. Having worked in the industry for a few years, I noticed certain aspects of the moving business that needed improvement. With a retail background, I know how important customer service depicts a business’s reputation. Which brought on the establishment of My Moving Pal. We want to bring a positive image back into the moving industry through our customer satisfaction. We are a local, Houston based moving company that has been serving the area since 2016.

Our motto is “Customer First” meaning we believe in customer satisfaction and going the extra mile at every opportunity to assure that our customers are pleased with exactly what they want and need. Our skilled workers are hired based on their tenure in the industry and an extensive vetting process.

We handle full service moves, including local, long distance and statewide moves. We also offer packing services if needed because we know how stressful moving can be. We’d like the opportunity to alleviate the stress and make your move as seamless and stress-free as possible. Whether it be the smallest or the largest job, we are 100% committed to fulfill any task at hand. Our goal will always remain to meet and exceed customer satisfaction.
